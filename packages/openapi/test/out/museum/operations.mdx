## Get museum hours

Get upcoming museum operating hours

<APIPlayground authorization={{"type":"string","name":"Authorization","defaultValue":"Basic","isRequired":true,"description":"The Authorization access token"}} method={"GET"} route={"/museum-hours"} bodyType={"json"} path={[]} query={[{"name":"startDate","type":"string","defaultValue":"2023-02-23","isRequired":false},{"name":"page","type":"number","defaultValue":2,"isRequired":false},{"name":"limit","type":"number","defaultValue":15,"isRequired":false}]} header={[]} body={undefined} schemas={{}}>

</APIPlayground>

### Authorization

<Property name={"Authorization"} type={"Basic <token>"} required={true}>

In: `header`

</Property>

### Query Parameters

<Property name={"startDate"} type={"string"} required={false} deprecated={false}>

The starting date to retrieve future operating hours from. Defaults to today's date.

<span>Example: `"2023-02-23"`</span>

<span>Format: `"date"`</span>

</Property>

<Property name={"page"} type={"integer"} required={false} deprecated={false}>

The page number to retrieve.

<span>Example: `2`</span>

<span>Default: `1`</span>

</Property>

<Property name={"limit"} type={"integer"} required={false} deprecated={false}>

The number of days per page.

<span>Example: `15`</span>

<span>Default: `10`</span>

<span>Maximum: `30`</span>

</Property>
